Our Services

Exploring the intersection of neuroscience, consciousness, and movement

Movement Research

Our foundational practice explores the relationship between cognitive processes and physical expression. Through structured improvisation and scientific inquiry, we investigate how the brain's perception of itself influences movement quality and decision-making.

  • Perception studies
  • Embodied cognition
  • Proprioceptive awareness

Performance Creation

We develop performances that translate complex neuroscientific concepts into accessible and engaging dance works. Our collaborative approach brings together artists, scientists, and technologists to create multidisciplinary experiences.

  • Concept development
  • Choreographic collaboration
  • Interdisciplinary integration

Workshops & Education

Our educational programs offer participants a unique opportunity to explore the mind-body connection through guided movement explorations and discussions of contemporary neuroscience and philosophy of mind.

  • Corporate workshops
  • University residencies
  • Community classes

Research Partnerships

We collaborate with academic institutions and research organizations to contribute to the growing field of embodied cognition and movement studies, offering our expertise in translating theoretical concepts into physical practice.

  • Data collection
  • Movement analysis
  • Publication support
